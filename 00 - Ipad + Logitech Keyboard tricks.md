Since you are using a standaloneô Logitech keyboardô with absolutely no trackpad, your hands are already in the typing position. You can jump between your developer tools without touching the screen by utilizing direct modifier key layouts. [1]ô 

Because many third-party Logitech keyboards lack a physical Apple "Globe" key, the shortcuts change slightly. Use these layout hacks for your specific workflow: [2]ô 

## 1. The Instant Toggle:ô `Command + Tab`

This is your primary tool.

- Hold downô `Cmd`ô and tapô `Tab`ô to instantly bring up the macOS-style application switcher overlay.
- Tappingô `Tab`ô moves the selector box right. Releasingô `Cmd`ô drops you instantly into the app.
- The Developer Advantage:ô If you are actively jumping strictly back and forth betweenô Blink Shellô andô Working Copy, a single rapid tap ofô `Cmd + Tab`ô will instantly bounce you to the previous app in milliseconds. [3, 4, 5, 6]ô 

## 2. The Native App Slide:ô `Command + Right / Left Arrow`ô [4]ô 

If you prefer the clean visual sliding transition of the 4-finger swipe, use the directional keys. [4]ô 

- Holdô `Cmd`ô and tap theô `Right Arrow`ô orô `Left Arrow`ô key.
- This forces the entire screen to slide to the adjacent active space, mimicking the screen swipe exactly. [4]ô 

## 3. The Pro Fix: Remap your "Caps Lock" to a "Globe" Key [2]ô 

If you want to unlock iPadOS's powerful system multitasking commands (like opening the App Switcher overview entirely with a keyboard shortcut), you need a Globe key. Since your Logitech model likely doesn't have one, you can convert your uselessô `Caps Lock`ô key into a custom Globe key. [2, 3, 7, 8]ô 

1. Go toô Settingsô $\rightarrow$ô Generalô $\rightarrow$ô Keyboard.
2. Selectô Hardware Keyboardô $\rightarrow$ô Modifier Keys.
3. Tapô Caps Lock Keyô and remap its action toô Globe. [2, 8]ô 

Once remapped, you can perform these actions from the home row:

- `Caps Lock (Globe) + Up Arrow`: Opens the native iPadOS App Switcher grid.
- `Caps Lock (Globe) + H`: Instantly sends you to the Home Screen. [3, 4, 7, 9]ô 

## Summary of Keys for Your Setup
